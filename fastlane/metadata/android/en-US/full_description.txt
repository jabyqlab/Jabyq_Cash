Send coins to a <a href="https://cashaccount.info">Cash Account</a> or Bitcoin Cash address with Crescent Cash.

Simple. Secure. Trustless.

Crescent combines the simplicity of traditional, centralized money apps with the security of trustless Bitcoin wallets.

Crescent Cash is a non-custodial wallet that allows for you to send Bitcoin Cash to other Cash Account users with their username, or to normal Bitcoin Cash addresses.
